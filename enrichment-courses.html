What To Expect

A typical session, from start to "can we come again?"

  • 1. Welcome & orientation — meet the team, get a safety briefing, and learn what aquaponics is all about.
  • 2. Guided system walkthrough — see how fish waste becomes plant food in a live closed-loop system.
  • 3. Hands-on activity — feed the fish, plant seedlings, handle compost worms, or assemble a mini system.
  • 4. Reflection & takeaways — a short recap connecting what participants did to bigger ideas about sustainability and health.
⏱ 1.5–3 hour sessions 👥 Groups of 10–40 🎒 Materials provided 📍 On-site or at our farms
