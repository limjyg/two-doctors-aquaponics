Two doctors. One belief: health grows from the ground up.

Two Doctors Aquaponics began with a simple observation from the clinic — that true wellness needs more than medicine. It needs connection: to food, to nature, and to each other.

Our Founders

Meet Dr. Lim Jia Yang & Dr. Wang Fei Fan

Dr. Lim Jia Yang has been refining aquaponics and micro-farming solutions for Singapore homes since 2015 — turning a personal passion for closed-loop growing systems into practical designs that fit even the smallest of spaces.

Dr. Wang Fei Fan, an ENT specialist, brings a clinician's perspective to the farm — championing the belief that "wellness extends beyond physical health to include mental well-being." For Dr. Wang, time spent growing, harvesting and learning outdoors is itself a form of care.

Together, they founded Two Doctors Aquaponics to bring the science of sustainable farming and the practice of holistic wellness to schools, families and homes across Singapore.
